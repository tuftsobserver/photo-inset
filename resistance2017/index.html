Starting this summer, I've started to explore body texture and closeup portraits with a focus on suppression and expression. Raised in a rather traditional family in a rather conservative culture, I've always been educated to be reserved and conforming. Emerging conflict between the new and old generation, however, is inevitable. Under the influence of especially western culture, the youths have grown much more open and learned how to ask/fight for what they want in a way that minimizes conflict. It's very interesting to see how people are exploring the ways in which they could express themselves that are not rendered too rebellious, and how people are experimenting more with their sexuality and gender identity. All of my photos in this series are taken with iPhone/digital camera, of myself and a very close friend.

By Roxanne Zhang
